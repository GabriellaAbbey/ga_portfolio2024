About The Project

Goal

This project was completed in two classes, web design and web development. We had to design and code a single scroll website, my client was a local business 'Taco Kellz'. Starting her stand when she was only 15 in 2020, Kelly Ramones had asked for a page where customers could lear more about her and the menu.

Research and Sketching

Young, fun, and pink! It was important to the client that her original theme stayed with her new website. I also wanted to help create a page that could grow with her and her business. An updated version her original brand idenity will drive the hungry customers to order!
