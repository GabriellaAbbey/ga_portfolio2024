Reimagining Tourism

A logo design for an international city that captures its spirit, arts, culture, and being.

Challenge & Content

Finding Fribourg

The goal was to create a new logo for a travel destination. Fribourg, Switzerland also known as the oldest medival town in the country has many cultural aspects to choose from when designing a logo.

Process & Insight

Research and Sketching

How does one capture Fribourg in a singluar emblem? From the gothic architecture with art noveau stained glass windows to the rich history of the city founder, it was a fun challange to incorprate a little bit of everything.

solution

Final Product

This final design shares the same qualities as the Pit Viper sunglasses; Long lasting, memorable, and unique.
